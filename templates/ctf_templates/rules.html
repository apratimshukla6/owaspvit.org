Rules and Guidelines

  1. Flag format is TOVC{…}, unless specified otherwise.
  2. Sharing flags or solutions during the CTF is prohibited and may result in disqualification
  3. If you find a bug in any of the challenges - please report it in the queries channel of Discord server.
  4. Register as an individual as this is a solo event.
  5. If you have any query regarding the challenge, post it in the queries channel of the Discord server.
  6. The challenge will be a docker(which will be hosted locally), so it is advised to have docker installed in your machines before the CTF. (Link to install docker - Here)
  7. The Leaderboard rankings are NOT FINAL as your writeups will be evaluated as well.
  8. The marking scheme is as follows with a maximum score of –
    • User Flag – 20
    • Root Flag – 20
    • Writeup – 30 (10 points each to screenshots, way to user flag and root flag).
    • Writeup - File name must be your username.
    • First writeup (After getting the user and root flags the machine) – 10
    • Best Writeup – 10
    • Rooting without using Metasploit – 10
  9. The top 3 individuals will be declared as winners.
  10. Keep a clean, positive competition.
  11. Don't forget to have fun!
