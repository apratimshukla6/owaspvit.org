Instructions

We are excited to have you onboard for our gaming event VALOWASP!
Please read the instructions carefully, then proceed to the registration page.

  • 1. Click on the "Proceed to Payment" button, and visit the vitchennaievents page.

  • 2. Follow the below step for each of the member of your team:

  • 2.1 If you have an existing account on "vitchennaievents", then log in, else proceed to creating a new account.
    2.2 In case you create a new account, you will receive your username and password on your registered email, use the credentials to login.
    2.3 After logging in, enter the required details and make the successful payment.

  • Note: The payment process has to be followed by each of the team members individually, but the below form has to be filled only by any 1 of the team members, once all the team members have successfully completed their payment.

  • 3. Once you have made the successful payment for each of the team members, visit back the VALOWASP site and click on the Register button.

  • 4. In the displayed form, enter the required details. Make sure to use the same email id as you have used for each member on vitchennaievents.

  • 5. Once completed, click on the "Complete Registration".

  • 6. Once you have successfully registered, you will receive an email from OWASP VIT by 04-09-2021.

  • 7. Don't forget to join our discord for further details and check out the VALOWASP section.

  • In case of any queries, do mail us at owasp@vit.ac.in.
    See you at the event!
